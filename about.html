Finding What?

This site has information about the research and activities of me, Brian Muller. I've been vacillating between the areas of computational biology, computer science, and data mining for a few years now. I am, however, always looking for science.

About

I currently work at Vox Media as the Director of Data Science, where I write code and do math (sometimes at the same time) in DC/NY.

Before that, I was CTO and Co-founder of OpBandit, Inc which optimized content on major publishers' sites across 7 countries. OpBandit was sold to Vox in April of 2015.

Prior to that, I ran the Data Science, Data Products, and Data Infrastructure teams at LivingSocial in Washington, DC.

Before that I worked for the Washington Post as the Web Director for Foreign Policy Magazine in Washington, DC.

Prior to that I was a research assistant in the Center for Computational Genomics at Johns Hopkins University in Baltimore, MD.

Before that I was a graduate student at the Medical University of South Carolina in Charleston, SC where I received a MS degree in bioinformatics.
